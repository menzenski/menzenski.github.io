About me

I'm a second-year MA student in the Department of Slavic Languages and Literatures at the University of Kansas. I'm planning to join the PhD program in Slavic Linguistics in the same department next year.

My research interests include Russian verbal aspect, verb classification, linguistic typology, North American indigenous languages, digital humanities, literary linguistics, and East European science fiction. These interests are fairly broad, and might seem disconnected from one another, but I like to think that many of them will come together in my dissertation in the next few years.
